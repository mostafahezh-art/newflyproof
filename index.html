Frequently Asked Questions

What is a proof of onward travel ticket?
A proof of onward travel ticket is a flight itinerary document showing you have a plan to leave a country. Many countries and airlines require this before allowing entry or boarding. Our tickets look identical to real booking confirmations.
Is this legal to use?
Our tickets are for travel planning and visa application purposes. They are clearly marked as itinerary documents. Always check the entry requirements of your destination country before traveling.
