Frequently Asked Questions

What is a proof of onward travel ticket?
A proof of onward travel ticket is a flight itinerary document showing you have a plan to leave a country. Many countries and airlines require this before allowing entry or boarding. Our tickets look identical to real booking confirmations.
Is this legal to use?
Our tickets are for travel planning and visa application purposes. They are clearly marked as itinerary documents. Always check the entry requirements of your destination country before traveling.
How quickly will I receive my ticket?
Instantly — as soon as payment is complete, your PDF ticket is available to download directly on this page. No waiting, no email delays.
Can I use this for a visa application?
Yes, many travelers use our tickets to support visa applications as proof of planned travel. The document includes real flight details, booking reference, passenger info, and airline information.
What airlines are supported?
We support all major airlines including Emirates, Qatar Airways, Turkish Airlines, EgyptAir, British Airways, Lufthansa, Air France, and many more. Over 100 airlines worldwide.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes — if you're not satisfied with your ticket for any reason, contact us and we'll issue a full refund. No questions asked.
